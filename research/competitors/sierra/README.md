## The one-line takeaway
Sierra proved the voice-agent market, published the playbook, and **publicly admits the gap** (text-with-reasoning ~85% vs realistic voice 26–43%; auth/spelling-over-noisy-audio is the #1 failure) — but they are an *application* company that **multi-homes transport/ASR/TTS/V2V because none is good enough alone.** Syrinx's opening: be the obsessively latency-engineered, provider-neutral, ownable **voice transport layer** they and everyone else actually need.
