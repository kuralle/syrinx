# Sierra.ai — Customers / Industry / Main-Site Cluster (Competitive Study)

Fetched 2026-06-22 via firecrawl (markdown). All 16 URLs returned HTTP 200; none 404'd or were empty.

Sierra is a conversational AI agent platform (founded by Bret Taylor, ex-Salesforce Co-CEO / OpenAI board, and Clay Bavor, ex-Google Labs). Positioning: "Sierra helps businesses build better, more human customer experiences with AI." Sells "Agent OS" — multichannel (voice, chat, SMS, WhatsApp, email, ChatGPT), 58 languages, outcome-based pricing. This cluster = 8 case-study posts, 4 industry/seasonal playbooks, 4 main-site pages.

## CASE STUDIES

### Change agents: Minted — ai-agents-in-action
- **Date/author**: Published 2025-04-24. Melissa Kim (co-founder & CEO of Minted), with Bret Taylor.
- **Thesis / positioning**: Premium goods (weddings, holidays, home) brand uses a Sierra agent to absorb seasonal holiday demand surges and solve the cost-vs-quality tension in customer care.
- **Specifics**: Company = Minted (premium stationery/cards/art/home). Industry = retail / DTC. Agent gives step-by-step guidance (e.g. uploading addresses for holiday cards), order-status updates, art recommendations matched to design aesthetic; connects to multiple support systems; summarizes customer conversations for real-time visibility. Channel = chat (no voice named). **Metric: "resolution rates upward of 65%"** — including time-sensitive issues like changing wedding invitations / ensuring holiday orders arrive.
- **Verbatim quotes**: "With resolution rates upward of 65%—even for the most time-sensitive customer issues, like changing wedding invitations or ensuring holiday orders arrive on time—Minted's AI agent is helping to drive their business." / "It's also played a crucial role in handling huge peaks in seasonal demand; few businesses experience a holiday rush quite like Minted."
- **→ Syrinx implication**: Seasonal burst-scaling + per-conversation summarization is a marketed differentiator; note resolution-rate (not deflection) is the headline KPI.

## CROSS-CUTTING NOTES FOR SYRINX

- **Voice is first-class, not a pilot**: Rocket (1M+ outbound dials/mo), Safelite (Scarlett voice concierge), Funnel (voice in 20+ languages), Brex (voice same quarter as chat), healthcare (voice across 30+ clinics), FS (outbound fraud calls). Multimodal "Agent OS" is the architecture story.
- **Headline KPIs Sierra sells on**: resolution rate (65–80%), containment (80%), CSAT (92–95%), conversion (3–4x), NPS (+33 / better-than-live-agent), handle-time/answer-speed (90% faster), time-to-live (<10 weeks).
- **Differentiators to counter**: outcome-based pricing; Ghostwriter (agent-building agent); Insights/Explorer (eval + latency observability); Agent Data Platform (memory/recommendations); hallucination-resistance claim; 58-language reach; ISO 42001 / FedRAMP / EU AI Act compliance; ChatGPT as a publish channel.
- **𝜏-voice benchmark** (linked across all posts, May 1 2026): Sierra published its own real-time voice-agent benchmark — "278 grounded customer-service tasks across retail, airline, and telecom... deterministic end-to-end task scoring with realistic, controllable audio." Directly relevant to Syrinx's voice-eval positioning; worth a dedicated fetch (`/blog/tau-voice-benchmarking-real-time-voice-agents-on-real-world-tasks`) — not in this cluster's URL list.
